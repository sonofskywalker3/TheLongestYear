[Mods] My time-loop mod, The Longest Year, is in beta: restore the Community Center within one year or the Junimos rewind you to Spring 1

BODY (markdown):
A roguelite take on year one. I just released the first public beta of a mod called **The Longest Year**, and I'm looking for players willing to help me balance it.

The premise: you have **one year** to restore the Community Center. Each season sets a minimum you must donate before it turns — and if you fall short, the Junimos rewind time all the way back to Spring 1. The catch that makes it a roguelite: you don't start over empty. The strength you earned can carry forward, so every loop you come back a little stronger, until one year you finally beat the clock and break the loop for good.

A few of the pieces:

- **Miss a season's quota → the whole year resets to Spring 1.** Make all four and finish the Center to win.
- **Your offerings bank power across loops** — at each reset you spend it on things that carry forward: skill levels, tool tiers, recipes, buildings, even a kept pet.
- **Weekly themes** give you a bonus paired with a liability to plan around (and you can lift that week's liability by donating all of its bonus items).
- **Carryover lives on your farm** — a book that tracks each season's goals, a Cookbook/Craftbook for keeping recipes, and a stash chest that survives resets.

It's a **beta** and I genuinely want feedback on whether the **difficulty, pricing, and pacing** feel right — which season wall hit hardest, how many loops before it clicked, that kind of thing.

Details: **PC only, SMAPI 4.0+, start a new save on the Standard farm** (the mod sets up the farm type and intro for you).
