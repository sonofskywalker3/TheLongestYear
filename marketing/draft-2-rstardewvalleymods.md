TITLE:
[Beta] I made a roguelite time-loop mod — restore the Community Center within one year, or the Junimos rewind you to Spring 1 and you start over a little stronger

BODY (markdown):
I've been quietly building this for a while and just put up the first public beta (0.9.2) — **The Longest Year**. It turns year one into a roguelite loop, and I'd love feedback from people who play modded.

**The idea:** each season has a minimum amount you have to donate to the Community Center before it turns. Miss a season's quota and the Junimos rewind the whole year back to Spring 1 — but you don't start from nothing:

- **Junimo Points bank across loops.** Donations earn JP, scaled by rarity and by how late in the year you give, and they persist through every reset.
- **You cash JP in at the reset** for carry-forward upgrades — skill levels, tool tiers, recipes, buildings, even a kept pet — so each loop you're a bit stronger than the last.
- **Weekly themes** hand you a paired bonus + liability to plan around, and you can lift that week's liability by donating all of its required bonus items.
- **Carryover surfaces** sit on your farm: a Bundle Log that tracks each season's goals, a Cookbook/Craftbook for banking recipes, and a Junimo Stash chest that survives resets.

Restore the whole Center inside a single year and you break the loop for good.

It's **PC-only, needs SMAPI 4.0+, and wants a fresh save on the Standard farm** (the mod handles farm-type/intro for you). It's stable in testing — what I most want now is balance feedback: do the **seasonal minimums feel fair**, are the **JP earnings/upgrade prices** right, and how many **loops before it "clicked"** for you?
