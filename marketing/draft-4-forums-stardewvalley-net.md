TITLE:
[Beta] The Longest Year — a roguelite time-loop over Community Center restoration (PC, SMAPI 4.0+)

BODY (BBCode):
[size=5][b]The Longest Year[/b][/size]
[i]Restore the Community Center within a single year — or the Junimos rewind the seasons and you begin again, a little stronger.[/i]

[b]This is a public beta (0.9.2)[/b], and I'm looking for players willing to help me balance it.

The premise: you have [b]one year[/b] to restore the Community Center. Each season sets a minimum you must donate before it turns — and if you fall short, the Junimos rewind time all the way back to Spring 1. What makes it a roguelite is that you don't start over empty: the strength you earned can carry forward, so each loop you come back a little stronger, until one year you finally beat the clock and break the loop for good.

[b]How the loop works[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Miss a season's quota[/b] and the whole year resets to Spring 1. Make all four and finish the Center to win.
[*][b]Junimo Points bank across loops.[/b] Donations earn JP, scaled by item rarity and by how late in the year you give, and they persist through every reset.
[*][b]You spend JP at the reset[/b] on carry-forward upgrades — skill levels, tool tiers, recipes, buildings, even a kept pet.
[*][b]Weekly themes[/b] give a paired bonus + liability to plan around, and you can lift that week's liability by donating all of its required bonus items.
[*][b]Carryover surfaces[/b] sit on your farm — a Bundle Log that tracks each season's goals, a Cookbook/Craftbook for banking recipes, and a Junimo Stash chest that survives resets.
[/list]

[b]Requirements[/b]
[list]
[*]Stardew Valley 1.6+ (PC)
[*]SMAPI 4.0.0+
[*]A new save on the Standard farm (the mod sets up the farm type and intro for you)
[/list]

[b]What I most want feedback on[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Difficulty[/b] — are the seasonal minimums fair? Which season wall hit hardest?
[*][b]Pricing[/b] — do JP earnings and upgrade costs feel balanced? What did you save for first?
[*][b]Pacing[/b] — how many loops before it "clicked"? Did carryover make later loops feel meaningfully stronger?
[*][b]Bugs[/b] — please grab your SMAPI-latest.txt (from Stardew Valley/ErrorLogs/).
[/list]
All the balance knobs live in config.json if you want to experiment.
