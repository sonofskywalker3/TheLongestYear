[WIP/Beta] The Longest Year — restore the Community Center in one year, or the Junimos rewind you to Spring 1

BODY (BBCode):
[size=5][b]The Longest Year[/b][/size]
[i]Restore the Community Center within a single year — or the Junimos rewind the seasons and you begin again, a little stronger.[/i]

A roguelite time-loop over the Community Center restoration. This is an early public beta ([b]0.9.2[/b]) — feature-complete for v1 and stable in testing — and I'm posting it here to find players who'll help me get the balance right.

[b]The idea:[/b] you have one year to restore the Center. Each season sets a donation minimum; miss it and the Junimos rewind the whole year back to Spring 1. But you don't start over with nothing — the strength you earned carries forward, so every loop you're a little stronger, until you finally finish the Center inside a single year and break the loop.

[b]What's in it[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Seasonal time-loop.[/b] Miss a season's quota and the year unwinds to Spring 1; make all four and restore the Center to win.
[*][b]Junimo Points.[/b] Donations earn JP — scaled by rarity and how late in the year you give — and JP banks across loops.
[*][b]Spend at the reset.[/b] When the year unwinds, you cash banked JP into carry-forward upgrades: skill levels, tool tiers, recipes, buildings, even a kept pet.
[*][b]Weekly themes.[/b] Each week grants a bonus paired with a liability — and you can lift that week's liability by donating all of its required bonus items.
[*][b]Carryover surfaces.[/b] A Bundle Log that tracks each season's goals, a Cookbook/Craftbook for keeping recipes, and a Junimo Stash chest that survives resets.
[/list]

[b]Requirements[/b]
[list]
[*]Stardew Valley 1.6+ (PC)
[*]SMAPI 4.0.0+
[*]A new save on the Standard farm (farm type and intro are handled for you)
[/list]

[b]Feedback I'm after[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Difficulty[/b] — do the seasonal minimums feel fair, and which wall hit hardest?
[*][b]Pricing[/b] — are JP earnings and upgrade costs well-balanced?
[*][b]Pacing[/b] — how many loops before it clicked, and did carryover make later loops feel stronger?
[*][b]Bugs[/b] — please include your SMAPI-latest.txt (Stardew Valley/ErrorLogs/). All balance values live in config.json.
[/list]
