0.10.0 — Season-end gate + loop-reset stability pass

A big reliability pass on the season gate and loop reset, plus fixes from beta
reports. Changes since 0.9.6:

Fixes
- Season-end gate, part 1: finishing every goal no longer occasionally resets you
  anyway. The item-donation ledger is now reconciled directly from the Community
  Center's bundle state at day's end, so a missed deposit can't read as a failure.
- Season-end gate, part 2: failing the 28th no longer advances you to the next
  season. Completing the bus-repair Vault on day 28 queued the overnight bus scene,
  which raced the loop reset; the rewind-doomed scene is now suppressed on a fail and
  deferred behind on a pass.
- Loop reset no longer presents the weekly theme picker twice and discards your first
  pick — you pick once.
- The bus-repair (Vault) money goal now counts on remixed-bundle saves. The Vault is
  renumbered under remixed bundles, so the hard-coded indices never matched and the
  gate could never pass; the indices are now read from your live bundle data.
- The Season Goals menu shows the bus-repair goal in the same style as the others.
- Artisan goods keep their identity and value through the Junimo Stash. A Smoked
  Legend (and wine, jelly, aged roe, honey, bait, …) no longer returns as a generic
  57g item after a reset.
- Villagers stay out of the abandoned Community Center during a run.
- The mine elevator locks on reset — you no longer keep access to floors reached last
  loop unless you bought the keep-elevator upgrade.
- Weekly theme goals accept either egg color: a "Large Egg" or "Egg" task ticks for
  the brown or the white variant.
- A tool upgrade in progress at Clint's no longer survives a reset as a free upgrade.
- Removed the stale vanilla "Rat Problem" quest — the Center is already open from
  day one, so the investigation quest no longer appears.

Changed
- The Junimo Shrine planning view now states that JP is spent when a loop resets or
  you win, not mid-run, so it no longer reads as broken.
- Each season is now guaranteed a special-weather day in week 1, replacing vanilla's
  always-on day-3 rain.
